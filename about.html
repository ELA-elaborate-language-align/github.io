Holding ourselves to a higher standard

Everything we do—from curriculum to assessments—is designed to make our classrooms places where children can truly thrive.

  • Parents rely on our teaching skills

    • We hire teachers who thrive in investing in CleverLand Future.

  • 21st century approach to education

    • Our education team leads the industry. They use the most up-to-date research to build our curriculum, a curriculum that empowers teachers to help kids follow their dreams—today and for years to come.

  • Our Mission


    • We started CleverLand because we wanted to provide exciting, innovative care for our children, in an environment that presented plentiful opportunities for play, interaction and development, at their own pace and in their own space, simply because we wanted them to have the best possible start in life.


    We created a nursery environment that is safe, and learning opportunities are always plentiful. We recruited staff with passion, enthusiasm and a never-ending commitment to the children. Parents were always reassured that their child(ren)' happiness, because their emotional needs are met, their interests are nurtured and their individuality is respected.

    Our Personel

    We continue to keep this type of excellence in all of our activities and there really is something about a CleverLander child that sets them apart. They are happy, sociable, confident, independent, creative and unique. They love to explore and try new things and are guided each and every day in all kinds of little ways.

    Skilful early years educators, provide a special place where children are the centre of everything, filling their hearts and minds with a sense of awe and wonder on their individual learning journey, while giving them the confidence to develop their own theories about the world.
